The Challenge Farmers Face

Late Detection of Crop Diseases

Farmers often detect crop diseases too late, reducing yields and impacting overall crop management.

Lack of Expert Agricultural Guidance

Access to expert agricultural advice can be limited, leading to suboptimal farming decisions and practices.

One-Size-Fits-All Recommendations

Generic farming advice doesn't work for every unique farming situation.

No Historical Tracking or Insights

Most farmers don't have access to comprehensive historical data to inform future decisions.
